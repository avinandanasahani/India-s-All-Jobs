❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is IndianJobsPortal free to use?

Absolutely! We charge no fee for any job listing. All information is freely accessible.

How often are jobs updated?

Our team updates the portal daily, sometimes multiple times a day, as soon as a new official notification is released.

Are the apply links genuine?

Yes, every "Apply Now" button takes you to the official career/recruitment page of the respective organization. We never use third-party links.

Do I need to register to apply?

No registration on our site is required. However, the official website may ask you to register for the specific job application.

Can I get job alerts on mobile?

We are working on push notifications. For now, you can bookmark our page and visit regularly. You can also subscribe to our newsletter in the footer.
