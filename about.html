Our Mission

At IndianJobsPortal, we aim to bridge the gap between job seekers and genuine employment opportunities. Every day, thousands of Sarkari and private vacancies are announced across India – our mission is to bring them all to one place, absolutely free. No fees, no hidden charges. Just pure, verified information with direct official apply links.

Who We Are

IndianJobsPortal was founded by Avinandana Sahani, a passionate tech enthusiast and career mentor who believes that every Indian deserves equal access to job information. What started as a small WhatsApp group sharing job alerts has now grown into a full‑fledged web portal serving millions of aspirants monthly. Our team works round the clock to update the latest notifications from SSC, UPSC, Railways, Banking, PSUs, IT giants, and more.

Why Choose IndianJobsPortal?

  • 100% free – no subscription, no premium charges.
  • Direct links to official career pages – no middleman.
  • Covers all categories: Govt, Private, Work‑from‑Home, Internships.
  • User‑friendly search & filter to find exactly what you need.
  • Mobile responsive: access from any device, anywhere.
  • Regular updates – we add new vacancies within hours of notification.

Our Team

We are a small but dedicated group of content curators, developers, and designers committed to making job hunting stress‑free. If you wish to join our mission, feel free to contact us.
